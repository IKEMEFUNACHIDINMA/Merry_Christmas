▎Animation Storyline

1. Scene 1: The Falling Gift
   – The animation begins with a beautifully wrapped Christmas present that gracefully falls from the top of the screen, capturing the viewer's attention with its colorful design.

2. Scene 2: The Stick Figure Girl
   – A stick figure girl pops her head from the side of the screen, displaying excitement as she spots the gift. She walks over to pick up the box, showcasing smooth walking animations.

3. Scene 3: The Family Gathering
   – The girl approaches a group of seven stick figures representing a family: a father, a mother, and five daughters. Each character has distinct features and colors to represent their gender and individuality.

4. Scene 4: The Gift Reveal
   – The girl places the box in front of a festive table where the family is gathered. As she opens the box, the words "MERRY CHRISTMAS TO YOU!" jump out in bold, colorful letters, dancing around joyfully.

5. Scene 5: Celebration
   – The family erupts in laughter and smiles at the cheerful display. They join in singing along to "Feliz Navidad," creating a warm and festive atmosphere.

6. Final Scene: Dancing Together
   – The scene closes with the family and the girl dancing together, celebrating the joy of Christmas. This joyful sequence repeats once more before concluding.

▎Features

• Smooth transitions and animations for a delightful viewing experience.
• Distinctive colors and features for male and female characters to enhance visual appeal.
• Background music that adds to the festive mood.

▎Technologies Used
